The Life and Career of Cassio Ramos

Cassio Ramos is a Brazilian football player born in 1987 in Veranópolis,- Rio Grande do Sul. Cassio is known for his amazing goalkeeping skills, more specifically - in the Brazilian club, Corinthians.

Early Life and Career

Cassio began his football journey in the youth system of Grêmio, where he developed his skills before moving to Europe to play for PSV Eindhoven. His early dedication and discipline were the foundation of a career that later made him one of Corinthians most iconic and successful goalkeepers.

Cassio’s Top Career Moments

Cássio Ramos achieved some of his top career moments with Corinthians, where he became a club legend. He was a key figure in their 2012 Copa Libertadores and FIFA Club World Cup victories, earning the Golden Ball as the tournament’s best player. Over the years, he also helped Corinthians secure multiple Campeonato Paulista and Brasileirão titles, solidifying his legacy as one of the greatest goalkeepers in the club’s history.

Clubs Cassio Played For:

  1. Grêmio - 2004 to 2007
  2. PSV Eindhoven - 2007 to 2011
  3. Sparta Rotterdam (loan) - 2008 to 2009
  4. Corinthians - 2011 to 2024
  5. Cruzeiro - 2024 to present
Biggest Titles Throughout His Career
  • Copa America (2018)
  • Brasileirão (2017, 2015)
  • Copa Libertadores (2012)
  • Mundial de clubes da FIFA (2012)
  • Recopa Sudamericana (2012)
  • Campeonato Paulista (2013, 2016, 2017, 2019)
